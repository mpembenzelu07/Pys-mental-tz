FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I know if therapy is right for me? +

Therapy can help anyone dealing with life's challenges. If you're experiencing persistent sadness, anxiety, relationship issues, or simply want personal growth, therapy can provide valuable tools and support. We offer a free 30-minute consultation to help you decide.

Is everything I say confidential? +

Yes, absolutely. All sessions are confidential under professional ethics and Tanzanian law. The only exceptions are if there's immediate risk of harm to yourself or others, which we would discuss with you first.

Do you accept insurance? +

We work with several insurance providers. Please contact us with your insurance details, and we'll check your coverage. We also offer sliding scale fees for those without insurance.

What's the difference between English and Swahili therapy? +

The therapeutic techniques are the same, but language affects how we process emotions. Swahili sessions use culturally-relevant metaphors and concepts, while English sessions use international therapeutic terminology. Many clients choose to mix both languages.

How soon can I get an appointment? +

We typically have availability within 1-3 days for new clients. Emergency sessions are available within 24 hours. After submitting your booking request, we'll contact you to schedule your first session.
